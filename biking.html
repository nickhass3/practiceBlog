Nick's Advice about Biking Seattle

Mountain Biking

Seattle is within a short day trip to many mountains. There are lots of great places to mountain bike or downhill bike.

  • Mt. Rainier
  • Cascade Mountains
  • Olympic Mountains

Local Trail Biking

There are a plethora of paved/gravel biking trails in the greater Seattle area. I think most people that are casual bikers purchase hybrid bikes that are great for both the pavement and the gravel.

City Biking

Seattle is one of America's most bike friendly cities. Road bikes are light and provide a great riding experience if you're using it to commute.
